# Human-AI Navigation
Exploring AI alignment, ethics, interaction, and NLP from a humanities perspective.

## About
Hi, I'm Kathryn - welcome to my repo! I'm a writer, researcher, and English MA holder with a background in teaching, library and archival science, and university administration. I'm currently writing a near-future, science-fiction novel based on the dawn of ASI, and through my research for this book, I became fascinated with the development of AI systems. This has led me to shifting my career into the world of AI training and development. I hope to use my unique background of humanities, philosophy, and linguistics, along with my practical pedagogical experiences, to add human-centred approaches to AI development, alignment, and interaction. I am also teaching myself how to code (Python, SQL), and I am embarking upon an ambitious self-study plan of mathematics, physics, and computer science (specifically machine learning). I have a lot to catch up on, and I'm very happy for any guidance that can be offered during my journey. I am a hopeful PhD applicant and always looking for work relating to my AI interests, so please feel free to get in touch if my skills and experience may assist you with any of your projects or work.
